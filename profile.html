Lore

This is the original Petr that started it all. First spotted on campus in September 2023, the Classic Petr has become an iconic symbol of UCI's sticker culture. Known for its simple yet charming design, this Petr can be found adorning laptops, water bottles, and notebooks across campus. The creator remains unknown, adding to the mystery and legend of this beloved sticker.
